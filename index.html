Welcome to UMSI Career Success Website

Ready to take the next step toward your future career? The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub is your one-stop shop for trusted guidance, practical tools, and the latest information to help you thrive as a student and job-seeker.

Quick Start

  1. Draft or update your resume (see Resume).
  2. Identify 5-10 target companies and 5 people to contact (see Networking).
  3. Practice behavioral and technical questions (see Interview Preparation).

Featured External Resources

  • O*NET Online - role descriptions and skills
  • LinkedIn Learning - short upskilling courses
  • Glassdoor Interview Reviews
  • Coursera - certificates to strengthen your profile

Suggested Structure

  • Header: Name, email, phone, LinkedIn/GitHub (as applicable).
  • Education: Degree, school, graduation date, relevant coursework.
  • Experience: Role, organization, location, dates, 3–5 impact bullets.
  • Projects: Links and results; show your contributions and outcomes.
  • Skills & Certifications: Group by category (e.g., Languages, Tools).

Core Principles

  • One page for most students; clear headings and consistent formatting.
  • Action-oriented bullet points using strong verbs and measurable outcomes.
  • Prioritize the most relevant experiences for your target roles.
  • Include key skills, tools, and certifications recruiters search for.

Export

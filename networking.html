Why Networking Matters

Building your professional network is one of the most important steps in career development. Whether online or in-person, every contact can open doors and provide valuable insight.

Tips for Effective Networking

  • Don't get discouraged by low response rates (average ~20% for cold messages).
  • Keep your outreach short and respectful of time.
  • Personalize your message to show genuine interest.
  • Never send your resume first—always ask for permission.
  • Focus on building a relationship before asking for referrals.

Crafting Your Message

  • Write a descriptive and enticing subject line.
  • Start with context that connects you to the recipient.
  • Be specific with your "ask".
  • Make it easy to say yes by suggesting a timeline (e.g., 20 min coffee chat).
  • Always say thank you!
